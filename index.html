A: Bed Leveling

I must point out one point at the beginning. There are many types of printers. The article is wrong; the correct version is: There are many types of printers, some of which do not have automatic leveling for the print bed, as shown in the picture below. In this case, the bed must be leveled manually using the screws located under the bed. We recommend using an A4 sheet of paper between the print head and the print bed to ensure that the print head does not touch the print bed.
